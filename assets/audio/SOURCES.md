# Audio Sources

The three recordings below replace the earlier procedural placeholders. They are resampled to 44.1 kHz, 16-bit stereo WAV and loudness-normalized for in-game playback.

## Sheep bleat

- File: `sheep_bleat.wav`
- Source: `Assets/Resource/Audio/Sheep.wav` from [GGJ-2026-Yedao-Bige/2026-Ciga](https://github.com/GGJ-2026-Yedao-Bige/2026-Ciga)
- Source commit: `d10c17897b660b0d9710b7a985afbe674016d74f`
- License: MIT, copyright 2026 Tonny Peng (repository license)
- Processing: high-pass/low-pass cleanup, loudness normalization, short edge fades, 48 kHz float PCM to 44.1 kHz 16-bit PCM

## Day and night ambience

- Files: `ambience_day.wav`, `ambience_night.wav`
- Sources: `30-cc0-sfx-loops/ambient_01.ogg` and `30-cc0-sfx-loops/ambient_03.ogg` from [lavenderdotpet/CC0-Public-Domain-Sounds](https://github.com/lavenderdotpet/CC0-Public-Domain-Sounds)
- Source artist metadata: `rubberduck`
- License: CC0-1.0
- Processing: repeated from the source loop to 32 seconds, filtered, loudness-normalized, edge-faded, and converted from 48 kHz Vorbis to 44.1 kHz 16-bit PCM
